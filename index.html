oakland.js

A weekly free + public hangout for JavaScript enthusiasts that happens in Oakland, California. No presentations, RSVPs or startup pitches/overt recruiting allowed, just show up and talk, learn and hack!

When & Where

Every Wednesday, 5:30PM - 8:30PM Lost & Found 2040 Telegraph Ave, Oakland, CA 1 block from the 19th St BART Station

This website is open source and you can fork it on GitHub. CSS was originally forked from the-gss.
